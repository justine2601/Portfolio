Chariz Solomon on Baby Andreas: 'Ikaw ang guardian angel ko'


Source: GMA | Aedrianne Acar
Posted at Mar 18 2021 | 12:00 PM

Chariz Solomon pens heartfelt appreciation post for her Baby Andreas.

Bubble Gang comedienne Chariz Solomon touched the hearts of many with her tear-jerking post for her son, Andreas Manolo.

Chariz, who announced that she's pregnant with her third child on her 31st birthday in August 2020, gave birth to Andreas on September 30, 2020 via cesarean section.

The versatile comedy star posted a sleeping photo of her baby on Instagram Story and wrote the caption, “Pinadala ka talaga sa'kin ng diyos hindi para alagaan. Kundi the other way around.

“Sa totoo lang ikaw ang nag-aalaga sa'kin.”

“Ikaw ang guardian angel ko Andreas. You are my strength and my reason for fighting. God loves me. At inupdate ako ni Lord about that through you.”

                

Mommy Chariz is currently in a relationship with club DJ Vince Teotico, while she has two sons with former partner businessman Nestor Ng, Apollo and Ali.
